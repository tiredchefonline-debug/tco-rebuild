What is Local SEO and why it matters

Local SEO aligns three signals so nearby customers can find you without ads: your Google Business Profile, your website content, and your citations. When these match, Google trusts who you are, what you offer, and where you operate. That trust shows up as more map impressions, direction requests, and phone calls.

Our process

  1. Quick audit — find indexing issues, category mismatches, duplicate listings, and missing content.
  2. Profile tuning — correct categories, services, hours, photos, products/menus, and Q&A.
  3. On-page alignment — service pages that match search intent with clear offers and schema.
  4. Citations & NAP — consistent name, address, and phone across top directories.
  5. Reviews & photos — a simple system to earn steady, authentic reviews and fresh visuals.
  6. Tracking & reporting — GA4 events and UTM tags so you see which channels produce leads.
